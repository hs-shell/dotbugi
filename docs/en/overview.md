## View Hansung University LMS Lectures, Assignments, and Quizzes at a Glance

This program aggregates lectures, assignments, and quizzes provided by the **Hansung University LMS** into a single view. Students can easily check attendance for each course and submission status for assignments/quizzes, and receive timely notifications as submission deadlines approach so that they don't miss them.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Unified Information

- **Lectures**: Course name, schedule, instructor info, etc.
- **Assignments**: Title, due date, details, etc.
- **Quizzes**: Title, schedule, questions and answers, etc.
- **Attendance & Submission Status**: Attendance for each course and submission status for assignments/quizzes

### 2. Deadline Notifications

- **Real-time alerts**: Notifies users of approaching submission deadlines for assignments and quizzes
- **Schedule management**: Helps users not to miss important schedules and deadlines

### 3. Google Calendar Integration

- **Credential management**: Securely stores authentication tokens and credentials for Google Calendar integration
- **Schedule sync**: Syncs lecture, assignment, and quiz schedules with Google Calendar for unified management

---

## Information Collection

### Website Content

This extension collects the following information from Hansung University LMS web pages:

- **Lectures**: Course name, schedule, instructor info, etc.
- **Assignments**: Title, due date, details, etc.
- **Quizzes**: Title, schedule, questions and answers, etc.
- **Attendance & Submission Status**: Attendance for each course and submission status for assignments/quizzes

### Specific Data Items

- **Lectures**
  - Course name
  - Schedule and timetable
  - Instructor and course introduction
- **Assignments**
  - Title
  - Due date
  - Details and requirements
- **Quizzes**
  - Title
  - Schedule and time limit
  - Questions and answer details
- **Attendance & Submission Status**
  - Attendance for each course
  - Submission status for assignments and quizzes

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- **Storage location**: All collected data is stored only in the user's browser or local storage.
- **No external transmission**: Data is not transmitted to external servers or third parties (including Google Calendar integration credentials).

### Privacy Protection and No Sensitive Data Collection

- **Minimum necessary information only**: Apart from Hansung University LMS web content and Google Calendar integration credentials, no additional personal or sensitive information is collected.
- **Strong security**: User information protection is the top priority, and strict security policies are observed during data collection and storage.

---

## Conclusion

This service helps students view various information from the Hansung University LMS in one place and easily manage submission deadlines and attendance. By integrating lectures, assignments, and quizzes with Google Calendar sync, it maximizes user convenience while data remains safely stored locally, ensuring privacy protection.
